panjang (λ) = 2 mgetaran (n) = 500waktu (t) = 2 s

Rumus f = n/t

f = 500/2

f = 250 Hz

(c) 250 Hz

......................................

Periode (T) = 1/f

T = 1/250 s

T = 0,004 s

......................................

Cepat rambat (V) = f×λ

v = 250 × 2

v = 500 m/s

......................................
