1 with g (specialist = a person who specialises in a certain field of study)

2 with d (valeting = cleaning services, mainly road transport)

3 with k (range = the space between two or more reas)

4 with b (upholstery = the soft, padded covering used for furniture)

5 with i (to thrive = to grow or develop well or vigurously)

6 with e (impeccable = without faults or mistakes)

7 with a (to enquire = to ask)

8 with j (premises = a place)

9 with f (customer = a purchaser or client)

10 with c (advantegous or helpful)

TASK 18

1. I like the boy. He always acts in a favorable manner.
