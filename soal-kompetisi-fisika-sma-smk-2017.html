[tex]\sf AC=\sqrt{AB^{2}+BC^{2}}= \sqrt{24^{2}+(24\sqrt{3}) ^{2}}=\sqrt{576+576(3)} =\sqrt{2304}=\boxed{ \sf 48\:cm}[/tex]
